Modern AI coding tools like Cursor, Windsurf, and [Agentforce Vibes](https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/developers/vibe-coding/) are changing how developers build. While prompting generates UI quickly, creating experiences people trust and love requires design thinking.

How do you collaborate with AI to build trusted, delightful experiences? Connect your AI tool to [Salesforce DX MCP](https://developer.salesforce.com/docs/atlas.en-us.sfdx_dev.meta/sfdx_dev/sfdx_dev_mcp.htm). This connection gives your AI deeper context and built-in SLDS 2 design system intelligence, automatically suggesting proven patterns, proper styling hooks, and accessibility best practices.

Think of it as having a design system expert sitting next to every developer, ensuring consistent, accessible experiences across all Salesforce apps.

**Benefits of building with AI and SLDS 2:**

- Generate up to 30% more accurate code.
- Prototype faster using natural language to translate your Figma design into SLDS 2-compatible code.
- Focus on building lovable experiences instead of learning design standards.
