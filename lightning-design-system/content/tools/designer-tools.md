## Design Tool

Figma is at the heart of Salesforce's design process. It’s where our UI libraries come to life, design explorations push boundaries, and team celebrations showcase creativity and innovation.

## UI Libraries

UI Libraries are your go-to resource for creating consistent, high-quality designs. They contain pre-built components like buttons, form elements, and icons, all designed to align with our product's visual language and interaction patterns.

To get started, open the UI library in Figma, drag and drop components into your designs, and begin prototyping. These scalable, flexible elements will streamline your workflow and ensure system alignment. Stay updated on library changes to take advantage of the latest improvements.
