Utility classes are ready-to-use CSS classes that make it easy to apply common styles to your components. With utility classes, you can quickly apply styles for spacing, alignment, typography, sizing, and visibility without having to write custom CSS. Utility classes help you streamline development, maintain design consistency, and reduce redundancy in your code.

## General

General-purpose utility classes.

## Layout

Layout classes keep product layouts consistent and predictable with various screen sizes.

## List

List utility classes manage how lists are ordered and positioned.

## Styling

Styling utility classes are used to manage the styling of UI elements in SLDS.
