## Action Icons

Action icons represent actions that users can take within an app on a touch device. These important icons, which represent the main ways Salesforce users accomplish tasks, appear only in the **mobile action bar**.

Action icons—close, submit for approval, filter, share post, new lead, fallback

### Anatomy

- Based on a grid of 48x48px
- 4px stroke weight
- 4px corner radius
- 4px negative space
