SLDS 2 powers how you as a Salesforce admin configure your company’s image on the platform with themes and branding. As an admin, your role is to oversee and collaborate with your designers and developers in your transition to or use of SLDS 2. If you’re transitioning your org from SLDS 1, read [Transition to SLDS 2](/get-started/transition-to-slds-2).

### Themes and Branding

A theme is a set of custom brand images and colors that you create and apply to your Salesforce org. Use themes to personalize the appearance of your Salesforce org for your team so that it aligns with your brand

To activate SLDS 2 in your org, turn on the default SLDS 2 theme, Salesforce Cosmos. Or you can create your own custom theme. After you’ve activated SLDS 2, you can also create an SLDS 2 custom theme. Upload your brand image and select your brand color. SLDS 2 automatically selects complementary accent colors from the SLDS 2 palette that meet[ Web Content Accessibility Guidelines](https://www.w3.org/WAI/standards-guidelines/wcag/) (WCAG) for color contrast.

To learn more about custom themes, check out these resources.

- [Brand Your Org In Lightning Experience](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=xcloud.brand_your_org_in_lightning_experience.htm&type=5) in Salesforce Help

- [Personalize Your Lightning Experience Display](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=xcloud.user_overview_lex.htm&type=5) in Salesforce Help

**ℹ️ Note:** Once you enable Salesforce Cosmos, or a custom theme, it activates for everyone in your org.

### Color Modes

As of Winter ‘26, you can activate dark mode for SLDS 2 themes in Starter Suite orgs. With dark mode (beta) activated, your users can select a color mode to customize their display preferences: light, dark, or system.

- By default, light mode is selected.
- After dark mode is activated in Themes and Branding, your users can switch between light and dark modes.
- Dark mode inverts the traditional light color scheme by presenting light-colored text and visual elements on a dark background.
- System mode matches the color mode that the user has selected on their device: light or dark.

⚠️ Dark mode is a pilot or beta service that is subject to the Beta Services Terms at [Agreements - ](https://www.salesforce.com/company/legal/agreements/)[Salesforce.com](http://Salesforce.com) or a written Unified Pilot Agreement if executed by Customer, and applicable terms in the [Product Terms Directory](https://ptd.salesforce.com/). Use of this pilot or beta service is at the Customer's sole discretion.

To get hands-on with dark mode:
