## Product Icons

Product icons use the branding for Salesforce products. Each product icon is made up of a two-color glyph with a 4px rounded stroke on a white background. These are used as mobile application icons. In Salesforce, product icons represent each official Salesforce application in the [App Launcher](https://v1.lightningdesignsystem.com/components/app-launcher/) as a 48x48px icon.
