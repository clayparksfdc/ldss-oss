## Shadows

All shadow styling hooks are prefixed with the namespace `--slds-g-` and are followed by the shadow name. For example,  **
--slds-g-shadow-1

 is the styling hook for a shadow depth of 1px.

SLDS global shadow examples showing four white rounded rectangles, each with increasing shadow depth: shadow 1, shadow 2, shadow 3, and shadow 4.
