## AI Assistant

AI assistants are agents integrated into a workspace, providing users with adjacent, on-demand support through a conversational interface.

Users interact with the assistant by providing prompts. These user queries enable the AI to create helpful, organized answers while inferring what the user wants, which makes it less likely to need highly specific commands. The AI recommendations and responses create smooth, natural interactions, enhancing productivity and decision-making.

Responses are displayed in structured feeds, allowing users to track, review, and act on the information efficiently. Past AI suggestions, user queries, and system responses are grouped and stored for easy access, ensuring continuity and referenceability. To maintain transparency and clarity, AI assistant updates include clear timestamps and authorship indicators, helping users understand the context and origin of each response.

## Embedded AI

Embedded AI includes enhanced form fields, action buttons, and cards that are added directly to the UI. It provides contextual support without disrupting the user experience.

Because AI has direct context with your work, it can surface relevant recommendations through in-stream UI elements like cards or menu dialogs. These suggestions are non-intrusive and fully user-controllable, ensuring that AI improves efficiency without overriding user intent.

Embedded AI cards highlight critical insights, recommended actions, or task summaries, enabling users to quickly assess and act on key information. To maintain accuracy and user control, a human-at-the-helm approach ensures that data is only modified after a human confirms AI-generated suggestions. This helps balance automation with accountability.

## AI Canvas

In an AI canvas, users can create, organize, and customize UI content freely within a workspace. The AI canvas is a highly flexible and interactive design experience.

Within this environment, components and their data are generated directly in the workspace, ensuring seamless integration and real-time updates. Humans can interact with the generated UI, modifying and refining it as needed to meet their specific requirements.

Canvases can be saved and shared, enabling easy access and continued iteration over time. Also, many users can work together on a single canvas at the same time. This helps teams work together better and makes them more productive by sharing real-time interactions.
