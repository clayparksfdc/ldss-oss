## Color Palette

Expressive palettes offer a wider range of colors than our System palette. Although their use varies, they can be useful for displaying data or highlighting important areas in your user experience.

Color palettes consist of cloud blue, purple, indigo, violet, orange, hot orange, red, and green.

- We recommend the use of mostly cool tones within your page designs.
- Indigo is similar to the brand blue (electric blue) color palette. Use it intentionally so that it does not interrupt the established button hierarchy.
- Warmer colors like orange, red, and green are only to be used to draw the user's attention to issues or important information.
- Use expressive colors sparingly to avoid confusion with the feedback colors: pink for errors, yellow for warnings, teal for success, and blue for info.
